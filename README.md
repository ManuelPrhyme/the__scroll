# The Scroll: Decentralized Writing

The Scroll is a decentralized platform on the **Somnia testnet** that enables writers to maintain full ownership of their work while earning Somnia tokens through direct subscriptions.

## Key Features

- **Direct Monetization**  
  Writers set their own subscription rates, and payments are transferred directly from the reader to the author via smart contracts.

- **On-Chain Ownership**  
  Articles are published on-chain, ensuring the creator maintains control over their intellectual property.

- **Seamless Reading**  
  Readers can browse the *explorer* tab to discover content and permanently access subscribed articles.

## Technical Integration

The platform utilizes **Somnia reactivity** to automatically synchronize the front-end interface with on-chain state modifications, such as new publications or subscriptions.

## Core Mission

The project's primary goal is to **decentralize content ownership**, allowing creators to manage their own writing and determine their own compensation without intermediaries.
